Service Description

At Aura Assisted Living, our Chronic Illness Management program is designed to provide personalized care and support for seniors living with long-term conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, heart disease, and other chronic illnesses. Our goal is to help residents manage their health, improve their quality of life, and enhance their well-being through carefully tailored care plans. We focus on prevention, early intervention, and symptom management, empowering residents to lead active, fulfilling lives. Our team of skilled caregivers works alongside medical professionals to monitor health, administer medications, and provide regular check-ups, ensuring that residents receive the best possible care.

Service We Provide

Our Chronic Illness Management services at Aura Assisted Living empower residents to take control of their health, manage chronic conditions, and enjoy a fulfilling life with the right care and ongoing support.

Our Chronic Illness Management services are comprehensive and focused on enhancing well-being and promoting health:

Personalized Health Plans
Medication Management
Dietary Support & Meal Planning
Physical Therapy & Exercise
Pain & Symptom Management
Regular Monitoring & Check-Ups
Mental & Emotional Support
