Types of Care at Aura Assisted Living

Compassionate & Personalized Senior Care

At Aura Assisted Living, we offer a range of specialized care services designed to support the unique needs of every resident. Our compassionate team ensures a safe, comfortable, and enriching environment where seniors can thrive with dignity and independence.

Assisted Living Care

We provide daily assistance with tasks like bathing, dressing, and medication while encouraging independence in a safe, comfortable environment.

Memory Care (Dementia & Alzheimer’s)

Our specialized memory care program offers structured routines, cognitive stimulation, and compassionate support for residents with dementia or Alzheimer’s.

Post-Surgical & Rehabilitation Care

Focused recovery care with physiotherapy, mobility assistance, and medical supervision to help seniors regain strength and independence after surgery.
