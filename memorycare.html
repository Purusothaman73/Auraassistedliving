Service Description

At Aura Assisted Living, we offer specialized Memory Care services designed for seniors facing Alzheimer’s, dementia, and other cognitive impairments. Our program provides a structured and secure environment where residents receive compassionate support, cognitive stimulation, and personalized care to enhance their quality of life. Our expert caregivers are trained in dementia care, ensuring patience, understanding, and reassurance at every step. We focus on minimizing confusion, reducing anxiety, and maintaining dignity, allowing residents to live in a safe, comfortable, and engaging environment.

Service We Provide

Caring for seniors with memory loss requires specialized attention, patience, and a deep understanding of their unique needs. Our Memory Care program provides a secure and enriching environment tailored to residents with Alzheimer’s and other forms of dementia.

Our specialized memory care services include:

Personalized care plans to address cognitive and physical needs
Secure living spaces designed to minimize wandering risks
Cognitive therapy and memory-stimulating activities to support brain function
Assistance with daily tasks like dressing, bathing, and meals
Compassionate caregivers trained in dementia care
24/7 supervision and emergency response for enhanced safety
Emotional support and family counseling to ease the journey for loved ones
